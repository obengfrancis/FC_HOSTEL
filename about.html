ABOUT


The management and staff of FCTC HOTEL, say a hearty AKWAABA! (Which Translates?) Welcome, to our new and unique facility. We are extremely glad to have you as our guest, and we take this opportunity to wish you an enjoyable stay.

ROOMS

We have 3 different types of rooms offering 38 guestrooms and suites each sophisticated with a touch of tradition and flavour to suit the comfort of our cherished guests. We have Standard Rooms (31), Executive Rooms (4), Suite (3) which are furnished with materials indigenous to the rich cultural heritage of Ghana. Guests are welcomed to splendid, conducive surroundings, some of which feature historic furnishings, precious fabrics and luxurious marble bathrooms. Once again we welcome you and we say enjoy your stay.
